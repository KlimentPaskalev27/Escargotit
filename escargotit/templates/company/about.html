About Us

Our History

We started our journey in 2010 as a small team passionate about snail farming. Over the years, we evolved into a leading snail farm management web app provider, serving thousands of farms worldwide.

Our Creator

Meet Kliment Paskalev, the visionary behind our platform. With a background in agriculture and technology, John embarked on a mission to simplify snail farm management through innovative software solutions.

Our Business Culture

We foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous improvement. Our team is dedicated to providing the best tools for snail farmers, ensuring their success and sustainability.

Environmental Footprint

We are committed to sustainability. Our cloud-based platform minimizes paper usage and supports eco-friendly farming practices. We are proud to contribute to a greener future.

How We Hire

We hire talent based on their passion, skills, and dedication. Our hiring process is designed to create a diverse team of experts who share our mission of empowering snail farmers.

Diversity and Inclusion

We celebrate diversity and inclusion. Our team comprises individuals from diverse backgrounds, and we actively promote equality and inclusivity in all aspects of our work.

Low Pay Gap

At our company, we ensure pay equity. We maintain a low pay gap and offer competitive compensation to all team members, regardless of gender, race, or background.
