Getting Started

Registering and Managing Your Snail Farm

1. Owner Registration

As a snail farm owner, the first step is to register your account:

  • Click the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page.
  • Fill in your details, including farm name and contact information.
  • Once registered, you'll have full control over your snail farm's data.

2. Adding Employees

Empower your team by creating accounts for your employees:

  • Log in to your account as the owner.
  • Navigate to the "Employee Management" section.
  • Click "Add Employee" and provide their details.

3. Granting Permissions

Control who can edit snail bed data:

  • For each employee, specify their role and permissions.
  • You can grant editing permissions for snail bed data selectively.
  • Ensure data security and streamline collaboration.

4. Statistical Insights

Gain valuable insights with interactive graphs and performance tracking. Visualize your farm's performance and make data-driven improvements to maximize efficiency and profits.

With our user-friendly interface, managing your snail farm and collaborating with your team has never been easier. Sign up today and optimize your farm's performance!
